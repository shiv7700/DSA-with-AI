# 06 — Sorting

> Sorting is the algorithm category most studied in computer science, and for good reason: it teaches every major trade-off — time vs. space, stability, in-place modification, and the limits of what's mathematically possible. Master the fundamentals here and you'll have a toolkit that applies to dozens of interview problems.

## Learn Path

1. **Read [`notes.md`](./notes.md) first** — why sorting matters, stable vs. unstable, comparison sorts, O(n log n) lower bound, full walkthroughs of Bubble, Selection, Insertion, Merge, Quick, Heap, Counting, Radix, and Bucket sort, plus JS `.sort()` gotchas and custom comparators.
2. Solve questions in order. Each lives in `questions/<NN>-<slug>.md`.
3. Write your solution in the matching `solutions/<NN>-<slug>.js`.
4. Tick off the checklist below as you finish.
